\newcommand{\longCipayes}[1]{% #1: Cipayes/Sepoys/Indiers
  \aparag[Availability.] \terme{#1} are Indian forces trained to fight
  alongside European armies. They can only be raised, and used, in
  \continentIndia, \continentIndochina and \continentIndonesia.
  \bparag It is not possible to raise \terme{#1} before the event
  \eventref{pVI:Last Great Mughals}.
  \bparag One \LD of \terme{#1} may be raised in each \TP\faceplus or
  \COL\faceplus each turn. A \MAJ having \terme{#1} may not have more
  than 5 \LD of \terme{#1} in play.
  \aparag[Logistics.] A \LD of \terme{#1} costs 5\ducats and its
  Maintenance is 3/1 \ducats as Veterans/Conscripts (disregarding if the
  \MAJ is at peace or at war).
  \aparag[Particularities.]
  \terme{#1} are \LD of the \MAJ, sharing its technology.
  \bparag \terme{#1} never cause reactions of Natives of or minor
  countries in the \ROTW. They are not counted for checking the
  conditions of reactions.
  \bparag \terme{#1} cannot be Incorporated in \ARMY counters.
}
